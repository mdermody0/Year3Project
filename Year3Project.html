Year 3 Project

Game 1 player character

Game 1 is about the player going to a beach, finding oil spills and using a cannon to absorb the oil enemies and clean up the beach.

This is the player character in an idle stance. I made this to help show that the game was still running in case the player thought the game had crashed.

This is showing the player character from a rear view while walking up the screen.

This is the first of three death animations for the player character. This animation occurs when the player has lost the last of their health. This particular animation is from the rear view.

This is showing the player character from a front view while walking down the screen.

This depicts the player character when they have taken minor damage from a front view.

This depicts the player character when they have taken significant damage from a front view.

This is the second of three death animations for the player character. This animation occurs when the player has lost the last of their health. This particular animation is from the front view.

This is an animation of the player recoiling from the power of firing the charged shot.

This is the third of three death animations for the player character. This animation occurs when the player has lost the last of their health. This particular animation is from the side view.

Game 1 Enemy Attacks

This is the melee enemy. It uses a hammer like attack from its own biomass.

This is the ranged enemy. It launches a projectile spike of itself at the player.

This is the bomber enemy. It gets close to the player, collapses in on itself and explodes out, firing shards of its body out.

Game 1 Enemy Movement

To show movement for this enemy, a claw launches out from its body and drags itself forward.

To show movement for this enemy, it rolls/somersaults across the screen.

To show movement for this enemy, multiple arms are used to drag the enemy forward with other tentacles swirling behind.

Game 1 Enemy Death

All death animations for the enemy characters are similar in nature. They all showcase what happens when the enemy is impacted by the charged shot.

Game 2 character

Game 2 is about cleaning up litter in the environment. The player character for the game is a fox that goes from place to place cleaning the rubbish it finds.

This is the idle animation for the fox. The tail twitches to show that the game is still running and hasn't crashed.

This is the movement animation for the fox. It shows the fox in a full sprint as opposed to a light jog.

Game 2 environment elements

These garbage piles would be found throughout the environment with pieces of the pile gradually being taken away.

Once the rubbish has been removed, a tree grows in the empty space. This shows how life is retuning to the area.

These signs show up ocasionally through the stages. The player can interact with them to see how many piles have been cleared.
